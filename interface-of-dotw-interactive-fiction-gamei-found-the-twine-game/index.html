I Found the Twine game!

David Rutland November 29, 2024

I previously mentioned that Day of the Worms was originally built as an Interactive Fiction game for Android - don't bother looking for it - I very much doubt it's compatible with any recent Android versions, if, indeed, it's still available.

I also ported it to Twine so that it would play in the browser. If you fancy giving it a go, it's available here. It's also somewhere in the menu.

These are the bones on which the point and click adventure is being built. It will have much of the same (possibly all of the same) text, but with hand drawn images, animations, and of course, it's a point and click.

Fun fact: the background image for the TWINE version is actually a photograph of my old plot on the Ilford Avenue allotments that I ran through GIMP.

I recall that some of the mechanics didn't quite work, but can't remember what exactly.
