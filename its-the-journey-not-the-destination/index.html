It's the journey... not the destination

David Rutland June 20, 2025

This is how you die. On your back, with a broken leg and worms crawling over and into your body.

Well, it's one of many ways you can die. Most of them involve trauma and worms. One will involve something approaching natural causes.

As with life, so with games. You're going to have to do a lot of things right just to stay alive, and even then, a good outcome isn't certain.

The destination is certain, but it's how you get there that counts.
