Meet Mike

David Rutland May 14, 2025

Scotch Mike is the first person you meet in Day of the Worms. He's your friendly neighbour, but right now, he isn't so friendly - and you may notice there seems to be something hanging from his nose.

Oh dear!

You'll need to negate the threat before you can move on.

You can burn Mike alive, scrape his flesh from his bones with a rake, or decapitate him with a spade. Do you kill Scotch Mike in cold blood or can you find another way to save civilisation along with your own life?
