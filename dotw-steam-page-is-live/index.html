DoTW Steam page is live!

David Rutland December 14, 2024

Sadly, you can't buy the game yet - because I haven't finished it, but the Day of the Worms Steam page has been approved, and is now live - which is nice, and I'm chuffed to have made it to this stage.

The actual process was pretty easy. I paid the dev fee years ago - when I was planning Day of the Worms as a pure Twine text adventure - and the page creation process mainly involved creating screenshots and library images of different sizes.

I'm going to carry on actually making the game in my spare time with an eye to a possible release date in June 2025 (edit from May 2025: "LOL"), and updating the Steam page with screenshots, descriptions, and so on. Of course, this blog will be updated much more frequently and continue to be the home to thoughts, development, images, and frustrations.
