Second area background art

David Rutland December 12, 2024

The second area is a path between the first and second allotment plots, and contains much of what you'd see on a normal allotment site.

There's a toilet with flowers, a bathtub to collect rainwater, weeds, a rather rough-and-ready fence, and a sturdy gate. Further objects will be added as... objects.

Beyond the fence, you can see the second plot - there's a polytunnel, various plants, and a water butt. The polytunnel will have its own interior (complete with tomato plants), and there will also be a bench.

The image is currently layers, and I'm not looking forward to separating everything out to create the walkbehind masks.
